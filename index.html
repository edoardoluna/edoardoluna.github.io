About

I am a fourth-year physics PhD student working in Professor Dave Thirumalai's group at UT Austin. My research focuses on stochastic interacting systems in biological contexts. Before graduate school, I developed machine learning models for climate dynamics under Professor Dimitrios Giannakis. I am also an avid poker player (Texas Hold 'em).
